Our Facilities

Ambition Convent School is committed to providing a learning environment that fosters academic growth, creativity, and well-being. Our campus is equipped with facilities that support a 21st-century education experience for every student.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive digital boards and audio-visual aids for modern, effective learning experiences.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for hands-on experiments and scientific exploration.

Computer Lab

Modern computer systems with internet access to enhance IT skills and digital literacy.

Library

A diverse collection of academic books, reference materials, and magazines to support reading and research.

Playground & Sports

Spacious playground for outdoor sports, physical education, and recreational activities.

Safe Transportation

School buses with trained drivers ensuring safe and convenient commuting for students.

Clean & Hygienic Campus

Regular cleaning, sanitized classrooms, and filtered drinking water to ensure student well-being.

Activity Rooms

Special rooms for art, music, dance, and creative exploration to nurture every child's talent.
