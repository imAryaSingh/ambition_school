Admission Process

Admissions are open for the academic year 2025-26. Parents seeking admission for their children are requested to collect the admission form from the school office or download it from our website.

  1. Fill the admission form completely.
  2. Attach necessary documents (Birth Certificate, Transfer Certificate, Aadhaar Card, etc.).
  3. Submit the form to the school office before the due date.
  4. Admission is subject to seat availability and merit.

Eligibility

  • Nursery: Minimum age 3+ years
  • Class 1: Minimum age 6+ years
  • Other Classes: Based on Transfer Certificate and age criteria

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Photocopy of Aadhaar Card
  • Passport-size photographs
